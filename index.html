About

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the University of Zurich. I was a visiting student at U.C. Berkeley in Fall 2021 and at Norwegian School of Economics in Fall 2022.

I will be on the job market in the 2023/2024 academic year.

My primary interests are in behavioral economics and public economics. I am interested in studying the implications of societal beliefs and preferences for the role of governments in societies. A better understanding of societal beliefs and preferences can guide policymakers in implementing policies that can improve social welfare.
